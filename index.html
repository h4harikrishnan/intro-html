Broke my trust on myself

Once upon a time..

You took me by hand, helped me pick up shattered pieces of myself. Let me believe, my hypothesis on trust is not true, and that trust is not an alien. Believing in you, I took my baby steps into the unknown world of love and trust, from the inhumane world of jealousy, self pride and unjustness. Before I could breathe, you pushed me into the deep, never to make it mack to you. Breaking my trust on you, inturn, broke my trust on myself.
